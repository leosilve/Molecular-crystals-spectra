Detailed Description

Periodic vector 3D class (inheritance from vector class).

It defines a 3D vector in terms of its integer coefficients n1, n2 and n3 over an orthogonal basis. The period is N1, N2 and N3, respectively in the three directions. It is used for the MultiPhonon basis set.
